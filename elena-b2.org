* Grammar stuff
| conditional | rule                       | structure                    | example                                               |
|-------------+----------------------------+------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------|
| 0           | facts                      | if + present + present       | Ice melts if you heat it                              |
| 1st         | possible future situations | if + present + future        | If it doesn't rain, tomorrow we will go to the beach. |
| 2nd         | unlikely future situations | if + past + would            | If we had a garden, we would have a cat.              |
1. I don't have a car, so I take the bus to work. \to If I don't have a car, I will take the bus to work. - FIRST CONDITIONAL
2. Don't walk on the ice. You might slip. \to If you walk on the ice, you will slip. - FIRST CONDITIONAL
3. The bakery is closed, so he can't buy bread. \to If the bakery is closed he can't buy bread. - ZERO CONDITIONAL
4. My advice to you is to go to the department store. \to If I were you I would go to the department store - SECOND CONDITIONAL
